Coin Operated Washing Machine

Category: Washer Extractors
  • Description
  • Technical Parameters

Description

1. The inner and outer drums of our coin-operated washing machine are all imported mirror stainless steel. The inner cage adopts the internationally advanced seamless spinning process, with no welding, the first in China, the strength is doubled, and it is durable.

2. Alloy material pulley, light and beautiful, super high strength. The grooveless V-ribbed belt design is more than several times the life of ordinary belts.

3. FMS suspension damping system, without any fixed, ultra-low vibration.

4. High quality Japan NSK bearings and shaft seals, longer service life.

5. The inner and outer drum gap is small, and heating and heating speed is fast, saving water and electricity.

6. The frequency conversion speed control can meet the speed’s linear climb and high-speed requirements, low noise and good stability.

7. The pipeline connection adopts the soft contact of imported anti-corrosion rubber from South Korea, which has small vibration, strong stability and doubled life.

8. The cage door is large, and it is easy to load clothes.

9. High-quality stainless steel heating tube, ring attached to the wall of the barrel, fast heat transfer, long service life.

10. Advanced drainage control system, accurate and easy to operate.
