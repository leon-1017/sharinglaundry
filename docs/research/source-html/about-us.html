Focus on Industrial and Commercial Laundry Equipment over 15 years

Company Profile

Brief Introduction

Wuxi Sharing Machinery Co., Ltd located in East China, 100km away from Shanghai, specializes in manufacturing and selling China’s best quality commercial and industrial laundry equipment, including washer extractors, tumble dryers, flatwork ironers, folders, dry cleaning machines, laundry press machine, and finishing equipment, etc.

We are acknowledged as a leading supplier in the field of industrial and commercial laundry equipment in China now. In the past 15 years, We have established long-term and mutually beneficial relationships with our customers at home and abroad.

Sharing’s laundry equipment is popular with customers at home and abroad, we always commit to continuing innovation & improvement, providing high-quality machines, professional & sincere service and competitive prices.
