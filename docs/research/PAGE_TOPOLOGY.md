# Page Topology

1. Sticky header with brand, primary navigation and product-category dropdown.
2. Homepage hero with primary message, CTA pair and category cards.
3. Trust/highlight band for quality, expert, price.
4. Introductory company section plus inquiry form.
5. Product range grid.
6. Partner / capability banner.
7. Testimonial grid.
8. Hotline CTA band.
9. News grid.
10. Footer with contact and social channels.

Template pages use:

- Hero band with title, entry kind and optional publish date.
- Main content column with optional lead image and migrated HTML.
- Right sidebar with inquiry form and original source link.
- Related content band for non-listing pages.
